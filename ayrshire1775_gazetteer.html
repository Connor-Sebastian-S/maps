A New Map of Ayrshire (1775): Carrick Gazetteer

The text of the Carrick portion of Armstrong's (1775) A New Map of Ayrshire has been transcribed as part of Ainmean Charraige/Carrick Names's data collection.

The entries below are linked to a georeferenced viewer for the map, which can be viewed and searched here.

We hope to add the text from Kyle, Cunningham and the surrounding areas in the not to distant future.
